Ref sheet details:
Dragonborn
Name: Alexis (Observer Aspect)
Pronouns: he/ him
sex: herm male
Hight: 1.6 meters was mistaken for rare kobold more than a few times
Self proclaimed mage, actually an Aspect of a greater dragon.

Main features :

Muscle feature not very pronounced overall androgynous, the give away is his thigh is thicker

Digitigrade legs with 4 Digits forward

Short white fur with no scales visible on body, a lot longer on the inside of ear and around neck also the back of joints, also have long fur that goes from back of the head to the tail.

No wings most of the time, only show up when angry, is made of blue flames and looks like bat wings.

Three horns two on the side and a smaller one in the middle with a side horn is broken and caped with jewelry, when angry the broken horn is completed by blue flame, and an eye will be projected in center of the horns.

Eyes: cat like and flame colored, when angry a lot of eyes of different sizes will be projected all around him.

Accessories: A lather bond "spell book" that's an extension of him, book can fly and cast spells independently.

Clothing: bands on wrist and ankles and a loincloth with bags and scrolls on the belt.

broken horn jewelry: looks like with flame with a cap to attach to the horn.
